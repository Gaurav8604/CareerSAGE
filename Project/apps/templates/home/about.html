Our history

Present

Career Sage is your dedicated compass in the journey of professional growth. With personalized insights, gamified assessments, and an intuitive voice bot, we make career exploration a seamless and enjoyable experience.

Sparking of Idea

Career Sage was ignited by the glaring need for proper guidance as students faced the challenges of selecting careers amidst misinformation and limited support.

Idea Implementation

Bringing Career Sage to life involves Bootstrap for a sleek UI, Flask as a robust backend, and Python's data science and machine learning for personalized insights. It's a dynamic blend, ensuring students receive accurate guidance and make informed career decisions.
